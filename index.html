Biography

I am a PhD student at Aalto University, Department of Computer Science, advised by Prof. Harri Lähdesmäki. My research focus is on Machine Learning methods for sciences. I have developed deep generative methods (diffusion models) on 3D drug-like molecule data, discriminative methods for protein sequences (in immunology and signal peptides), and peptide generative methods.
